Part 4 Evaluation Criteria & Rating Scale
Rating Score Range Description Action Required
Excellent 90 - 100 Consistently exceeds targets. Scripts are high-quality, on-time, and show strong concept thinking. Recognize; consider for growth opportunities or senior responsibilities.
Good 75 - 89 Meets most targets. Reliable output with occasional standout work. Minor gaps in ideation or quality. Positive reinforcement; set stretch goals for next period.
Needs Improvement 60 - 74 Partially meets targets. Volume or quality inconsistent. Concept ideation below expectation. Coaching plan; biweekly check-ins; set clearer deliverables.
Below Standard Below 60 Consistently misses targets. Significant quality or volume issues. Requires immediate intervention. Performance improvement plan (PIP); weekly monitoring.
